Frequently asked Questions

How can I book a ride with Chimjoy Car Rentals?

To book a ride, you can visit our website or contact our customer support team. Provide your trip details, and our agent will assist you in making a reservation.

Is online payment required to book a ride with Chimjoy?

No, online payment is not required. After confirming your booking with our agent, you can make your payment in person or using the payment method agreed upon during your conversation..

How can I modify or cancel my ride reservation?

If you need to modify or cancel your ride, please contact our customer support team as soon as possible. We understand that plans can change, and we'll assist you in making the necessary adjustments.

Is there a customer support team available?

Yes, our customer support team is available 24/7 to assist you. If you have any questions or face any issues during your ride, please reach out, and we'll provide the necessary support

How far in advance should I book a ride with Chimjoy Car Rentals?

We recommend booking your ride in advance, especially for specific events or busy travel periods, to secure availability. However, our team will do their best to accommodate last-minute requests whenever possible.

What documents are required for renting a vehicle?

You will generally need a valid driver's license, proof of insurance, and a valid payment method (credit card). International renters may need additional documentation.

Can I cancel or modify my reservation?

Yes of course but you just need to take a few steps by firstly contact the support and you will be guided

How can I contact customer support if I have further questions or issues?

You can reach our customer support team via phone, email, or our website's contact form. We are here to assist you with any inquiries or concerns.
